How Spotify Audio Features Map to Emotional States

HAPPY
Audio signature: valence > 0.7, energy > 0.7
Characteristics: uplifting, positive, celebratory. High tempo (120+ BPM typical).
High danceability. Common in pop, latin, and EDM. Songs with high valence and energy
feel joyful and emotionally uplifting. Examples: dance pop, latin pop, upbeat EDM.

INTENSE
Audio signature: valence < 0.3, energy > 0.7
Characteristics: aggressive, powerful, cathartic. Low valence creates tension while
high energy drives urgency. Common in rock, metal subgenres, and high-energy EDM.
The emotional state combines raw power with emotional darkness. BPM is typically 130–170.

SAD / MELANCHOLIC
Audio signature: valence < 0.3, energy < 0.4
Characteristics: contemplative, sorrowful, introspective. Slow tempo (below 90 BPM),
high acousticness, minimal percussion. Low energy creates space for emotional reflection.
Common in acoustic singer-songwriter, indie folk, and slow R&B. These tracks often feature
piano and acoustic guitar as primary instruments.

CHILL
Audio signature: energy < 0.4, acousticness > 0.5
Characteristics: relaxed, warm, unhurried. Moderate to high acousticness with soft
instrumentation. Tempo is slow to mid (65–100 BPM). Valence is typically neutral to
slightly positive (0.4–0.7). Ideal for background listening, reading, casual conversations.
Common in acoustic pop, lo-fi, jazz, and folk.

FOCUSED
Audio signature: 0.4 ≤ energy ≤ 0.7, valence ≥ 0.4
Characteristics: steady, consistent, non-distracting. Mid-range energy prevents distraction
while providing enough momentum to sustain attention. Moderate valence avoids emotional
peaks that break concentration. High instrumentalness is a bonus — fewer lyrics means less
cognitive competition with reading or coding. Tempo: 80–120 BPM.

PARTY
Audio signature: danceability > 0.8
Characteristics: groove-driven, high danceability regardless of valence or energy.
Strong rhythmic patterns, pronounced bass, and consistent beat make these ideal for
social settings. Common in EDM, latin, pop, and hip-hop.

AMBIENT
Audio signature: energy < 0.3
Characteristics: atmospheric, spacious, texturally rich. Very low energy, often minimal
percussion. Functions as a sonic background rather than focal music. High acousticness or
electronic texture. Tempo below 80 BPM. Used for sleep, meditation, deep focus.

RELAXED
Audio signature: all other combinations
Characteristics: comfortable middle ground — not energetic enough to drive action but
not so low-energy as to induce sleep. Moderate valence. Serves as catch-all for tracks
that have balanced audio profiles without extreme values in any dimension.

VALENCE QUICK REFERENCE
0.0–0.2: Very sad, dark, or angry
0.2–0.4: Melancholic, somber
0.4–0.6: Neutral, bittersweet
0.6–0.8: Pleasant, positive
0.8–1.0: Joyful, euphoric, uplifting
